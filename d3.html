                    Civil Engineering

Vision :

The Civil Engineering department envisions to build a strong teaching environment to achieve excellence in technical education providing highest standards of learning that transforms steadily to meet the challenges of 21st century.

Mission :

The department’s mission is:-

  • To provide an open end environment that stimulates professional and personal growth of Proximate Stake holders i.e. students and faculty;
  • To make our students competent enough in the fields of their career by giving more emphasis on Practical, Industrial, Computational         aspects etc.;
  • To develop Knowledge in multi areas like Management, Design and Computer applications;
  • To prepare leaders for diversified field of highly technological and information based Global Society;
